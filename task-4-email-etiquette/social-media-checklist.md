Responsible Social Media Use – Checklist
✅ Do’s

✔️ Think before you post—ensure your content is respectful and appropriate

✔️ Maintain a professional profile (good photo, clean bio)

✔️ Use privacy settings to control who can see your information

✔️ Share useful and positive content (educational, achievements, skills)

✔️ Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation

❌ Don’ts

❌ Don’t share personal or sensitive information (address, phone number, passwords)

❌ Don’t post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content

❌ Don’t engage in online arguments or cyberbullying

❌ Don’t accept requests or messages from unknown or suspicious accounts

❌ Don’t spend excessive time on social media that affects studies or health
