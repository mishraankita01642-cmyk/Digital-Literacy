🛡️ Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students (India)
✅ General Safety Tips

✔️ Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts (mix of letters, numbers, symbols)

✔️ Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps

✔️ Avoid clicking on unknown links or attachments in emails, SMS, or WhatsApp

✔️ Keep your devices updated with the latest software and security patches

✔️ Use only trusted Wi-Fi networks; avoid logging into sensitive accounts on public Wi-Fi

✔️ Check website security—look for “https://” before entering personal details

✔️ Limit what you share on social media (location, phone number, personal info)

✔️ Download apps only from official app stores (Google Play Store / Apple App Store)

💰 Financial / UPI Safety Tips

✔️ Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone—even if they claim to be from the bank

✔️ Remember: You don’t need a PIN to receive money—only to send it

✔️ Verify payment requests carefully before approving—check the sender’s name

✔️ Avoid scanning unknown QR codes or accepting random “collect requests”
